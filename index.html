Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city located in the southern part of Maharashtra, close to Karnataka and Telangana.

The city is known for its spiritual centers, historic monuments, traditional festivals, and vibrant culture.

Many famous spiritual places such as Siddheshwar Temple, Pandharpur, and Akkalkot attract millions of devotees every year.

Solapur is also known for its textiles like Solapur chaddars and towels.

This website introduces places, culture, travel info, and food of Solapur.
